Adding the Submission to TablePress

  1. Log into the commons.
  2. Click here to reach our table
  3. Scroll down until you see the end of the table.
  4. Add a row using the button on the left within "Table Manipulation"
  5. Populate the new row
  6. To add a new link, click "Insert Link" and follow the instructions. Link Text is what you want to appear in the table.
